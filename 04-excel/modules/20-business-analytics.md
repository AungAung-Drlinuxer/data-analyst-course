# Module 20: Business Analytics With Excel

## 📌 Business Analytics ဆိုတာဘာလဲ?
Business Data တွေကိုခွဲခြမ်းစိတ်ဖြာပြီး Decision Making အတွက်အသုံးပြုတဲ့ Process ပါ။

## BI vs BA (Business Intelligence vs Business Analytics)

| | BI (Business Intelligence) | BA (Business Analytics) |
|--|---------------------------|------------------------|
| **Focus** | What happened? | Why & What will happen? |
| **Time** | Past & Present | Future |
| **Tools** | Reports, Dashboards | Statistics, Models |
| **Output** | Descriptive | Predictive & Prescriptive |

## Four Types of Analytics

```
1. Descriptive Analytics (ဘာဖြစ်ခဲ့လဲ?)
   → Sales Reports, Dashboard KPIs

2. Diagnostic Analytics (ဘာကြောင့်ဖြစ်ခဲ့လဲ?)
   → Root cause analysis, Why did sales drop?

3. Predictive Analytics (ဘာဖြစ်လာမလဲ?)
   → Forecasting, Trend Analysis, Regression

4. Prescriptive Analytics (ဘာလုပ်သင့်လဲ?)
   → Optimization, What-If Analysis, Simulation
```

## ✅ Key Takeaways
- BA က future prediction အတွက်, BI က past reporting အတွက်
- Analytics 4 မျိုး: Descriptive → Diagnostic → Predictive → Prescriptive
- Data က ဆုံးဖြတ်ချက်တွေအတွက်အခြေခံအကျဆုံး
- Excel က Business Analytics အတွက်အဓိက tool တစ်ခုဖြစ်ဆဲ
